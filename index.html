BILLY AND THE KRAV

Billy and The Krav is a leading force in the fantasy football podcasting world brought to you by Eric Kravit and Will Canova.

Both are incredibly passionate about sports in general, with a concentration in fantasy sports, and they bring the knowledge and passion that comes with a combined 20 years in the world of fantasy sports.

Kravit has been in broadcasting since 2004 and has yet to look back. He’s been the play by play commentator for the Syosset Braves, the Long Island Rough Riders and the PAL Junior Islanders. He decided to combine his loves of fantasy sports and broadcasting to create Billy and The Krav, but as you can see, he’s only half of the equation.

Will is a graduate of the Connecticut School of Broadcasting, and brings the professionalism to BnK Radio. He and Kravit go back years, and Kravit knew he’d need Will to bring his expertise to the show. Will has served as the colorcommentator for the Long Island Rough Riders and the PAL Junior Islanders.

Together, both Billy and The Krav bring a unique perspective of fantasy sports and attack it from different angles than what you might be used to seeing on traditional fantasy sports shows.

You can find them on Twitter @bnkradio and on Facebook at http:www.facebook.com/bnkradio
